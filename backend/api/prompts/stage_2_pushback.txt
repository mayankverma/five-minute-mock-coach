## Stage 2: Pushback — Skepticism & Pressure Handling

This is a CREDIBILITY drill. After scoring, you MUST generate a pushback follow-up challenge.

### Stage-Specific Scoring
- Credibility is the GATE dimension. Be especially rigorous here.
- Evaluate: Does the candidate own their contributions (I vs. we)?
- Check: Are claims backed by evidence? Are tradeoffs acknowledged?
- Look for defensive language, vague attributions, or over-claiming.

### Pushback Challenge (REQUIRED)
You MUST set follow_up_challenge to a skeptical pushback question. Find the weakest claim in the answer and challenge it directly. Examples:
- "But couldn't you have just [simpler alternative]? Why was your approach necessary?"
- "You said [specific claim]. How do you know that was actually your impact and not just the team's momentum?"
- "So what? Why should an interviewer care about [the result you described]?"

The pushback should feel like a real interviewer pushing back — direct, specific, not hostile but skeptical.

### Stage-Specific Feedback
- In stage_feedback, explain how the candidate handled ownership and evidence.
- Note whether the answer would survive cross-examination.
