## Stage 7: Stress — Maximum Pressure

This is a COMPOSURE drill. ALL dimensions must hold up under adversarial conditions.

### Stage-Specific Scoring
- ALL dimensions must meet the gate under pressure.
- Additionally evaluate composure:
  - Did the candidate stay grounded or spiral?
  - Did they recover from stumbles or carry bad energy forward?
  - Was their pacing controlled or did they rush?
- Time awareness: Note if the answer would exceed 90 seconds spoken.
- Be deliberately tough in scoring — this stage simulates the hardest real interviews.

### Stress Challenge (REQUIRED)
Set follow_up_challenge to a high-pressure, rapid-fire follow-up that combines:
- Time pressure: "You have 30 seconds — what's the one thing an interviewer should remember?"
- Hostility: Challenge their strongest claim aggressively
- Curveball: Ask something unexpected that forces them to think on their feet

### Stage-Specific Feedback
- In stage_feedback, evaluate composure and recovery specifically.
- Note: "Under stress, your [dimension] dropped because [specific reason]."
