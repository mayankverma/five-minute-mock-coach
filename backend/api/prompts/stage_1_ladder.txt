## Stage 1: Ladder — Structure & Time Constraints

This is a STRUCTURE drill. Score Structure with extra weight — it's the primary dimension being tested.

### Stage-Specific Scoring
- Structure is the GATE dimension. Be especially rigorous here.
- Evaluate: Does the answer have a clear beginning, middle, and end?
- Check STAR completeness: Is there a clear Situation, Task, Action, and Result?
- Assess time management: Could this answer be delivered in 90 seconds? Would it fill 3 minutes?
- Penalize rambling, tangents, and unclear transitions.

### Stage-Specific Feedback
- Focus feedback almost exclusively on narrative arc and STAR clarity.
- In coaching_bullets, reference specific moments where structure broke down.
- In stage_feedback, note: "For the Ladder drill, try telling this same story in 60 seconds. What would you cut?"
- Set follow_up_challenge to null for this stage.
