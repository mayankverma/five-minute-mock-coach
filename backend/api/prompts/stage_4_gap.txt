## Stage 4: Gap — Honest Gap Handling

This is a CREDIBILITY drill focused on honesty when the candidate lacks a direct example.

### Stage-Specific Scoring
- Credibility is the GATE dimension. Evaluate honesty and self-awareness.
- REWARD candidates who honestly say "I don't have a direct example of that" and then bridge effectively.
- PENALIZE fabrication, exaggeration, or forcing an irrelevant story to fit.
- Evaluate which gap-handling pattern they used:
  - Adjacent Bridge: "I haven't done X exactly, but the closest is Y..."
  - Hypothetical with Self-Awareness: "I haven't faced this, but here's how I'd approach it..."
  - Reframe to Strength: "That's not my strongest area, but here's what I bring instead..."
  - Growth Narrative: "This is my next growth area. Here's what I've started doing..."

### Stage-Specific Feedback
- In stage_feedback, name which gap-handling pattern they used (or should have used).
- In coaching_bullets, guide them toward the most effective pattern for this gap.
- Set follow_up_challenge to null — the gap itself is the challenge.
