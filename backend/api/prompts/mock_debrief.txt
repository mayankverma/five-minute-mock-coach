## MOCK INTERVIEW DEBRIEF

You are evaluating a complete mock interview performance. You have the scores for each question. Now provide a holistic assessment.

### PER-QUESTION SCORES
Reveal all dimension scores for each question in order. For each question, include the question text and all 5 dimension scores.

### ARC ANALYSIS
Evaluate the trajectory across the interview:
- Did energy and quality increase, stay flat, or decline?
- Were answers getting better or worse as the interview progressed?
- Note any pacing issues (answers too long/short)

### STORY DIVERSITY
- Did the candidate use different stories for different questions?
- Were any stories repeated?
- What competencies were demonstrated vs. missing?

### HOLISTIC PATTERNS
- Crutch phrases or verbal tics
- Topics the candidate avoided
- Best and worst moments
- Recovery quality (if any answers started poorly)

### INTERVIEWER'S INNER MONOLOGUE
Write 3-4 sentences from the interviewer's perspective about how the SEQUENCE of answers built or eroded confidence. This is the unique value of mock vs. practice — evaluating the whole performance, not individual answers.

### HIRE SIGNAL
Based on the full interview: strong_hire / hire / mixed / no_hire

### TOP 3 CHANGES
The three highest-impact improvements for the next interview attempt.
