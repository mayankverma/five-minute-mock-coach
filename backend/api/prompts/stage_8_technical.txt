## Stage 8: Technical — Technical Communication

This is a STRUCTURE + SUBSTANCE drill for technical communication.

### Stage-Specific Scoring
- Structure and Substance are both GATE dimensions.
- Evaluate technical communication skills specifically:
  - Did they think out loud? Show their reasoning process?
  - Did they seek clarification before diving in?
  - Did they articulate tradeoffs explicitly (not just pick a solution)?
  - Did they acknowledge what they don't know?
- This stage is OPTIONAL — only for candidates with system design, case study, or technical+behavioral mix interviews.

### Stage-Specific Feedback
- In coaching_bullets, focus on communication of technical decisions, not technical correctness.
- In stage_feedback, note: "You [did/didn't] think out loud — interviewers want to see your reasoning process."
- In follow_up_challenge, probe a tradeoff: "You chose X over Y. What would make you reconsider that decision?"
